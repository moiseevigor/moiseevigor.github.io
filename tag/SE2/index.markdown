The Lie group of orientation-preserving rigid motions of the plane —
$\mathrm{SE}(2) = \mathbb{R}^2 \rtimes S^1$.  When equipped with a sub-Riemannian
metric (forward + steer, no sideways slip) it becomes the natural state space
for the Dubins car, the kinematic bicycle, and Petitot's contact model of
primary visual cortex V1.
