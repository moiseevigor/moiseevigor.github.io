The tangent space at the identity of a Lie group, equipped with the
commutator bracket — for matrix groups, $[X, Y] = XY - YX$.  The Lie
algebra $\mathfrak{se}(2)$ is the workhorse of the SE(2) sub-Riemannian
geometry behind Petitot's V1 model.
