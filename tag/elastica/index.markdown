The shape a thin elastic rod takes under terminal forces — Euler (1744)
showed they are the extremals of $\int \kappa^2\,ds$.  Their curvature is a
Jacobi sn, cn, or dn function of arc length; the inflectional family arises
in optimal control on $\mathrm{SE}(2)$ and in models of visual completion.
