Integrals of the form $\int R(t,\sqrt{P(t)})\,dt$ with $P$ a cubic or quartic.
Legendre's three kinds, Carlson's symmetric forms, and the special values
$K(m)$, $E(m)$ that govern the period of the nonlinear pendulum and the spatial
period of Euler's elastica.
