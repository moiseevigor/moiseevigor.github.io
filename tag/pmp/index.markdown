Pontryagin's Maximum Principle (1962) recasts an optimal-control problem
as a Hamiltonian system on the cotangent bundle of the state manifold —
the workhorse for sub-Riemannian geodesic computations and the
SE(2) elastica problem of Petitot/Sachkov/Moiseev.
