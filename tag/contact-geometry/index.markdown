A contact structure on an odd-dimensional manifold is the kernel of a
1-form $\alpha$ with $\alpha \wedge (d\alpha)^n \neq 0$ — the maximally
non-integrable distribution.  Petitot's V1 model recasts the visual cortex
as a contact 3-manifold $\mathrm{SE}(2)$ on which contour completion is a
geodesic problem.
