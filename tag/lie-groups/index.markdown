A Lie group is a smooth manifold that is also a group, with smooth
multiplication and inversion.  Examples: $S^1$, $\mathrm{SO}(3)$, and the
group of rigid motions $\mathrm{SE}(2)$ of the plane that organises
optimal contour completion in primary visual cortex V1.
