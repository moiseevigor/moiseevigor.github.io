Hi there! I'm an applied mathematician and artificial intelligence practitioner with a passion for exploring the intersection of human and machine intelligence.

I hold a MSc in Applied Mathematics and am currently working as a Director of Engineering at Standard.AI, where I focus on developing machine learning algorithms for various applications.

In my spare time, I enjoy maintaining the Elliptic functions for Matlab package, which is used by researchers and practitioners in various fields to solve mathematical problems that involves elliptic functions and elliptic integrals. I have also contributed to several other open-source projects, which can be found on my GitHub profile (github.com/moiseevigor).

My work has been published in leading journals such as ESAIM: Control, Optimisation and Calculus of Variations (ESAIM: COCV) and Molecular Pharmacology (Mol. Pharmacol.).

When I'm not working, you can find me crossing countries on foot or on my bicycle, reading science fiction books.

In addition, I am also an avid presenter
